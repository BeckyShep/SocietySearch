About

Welcome to Society Search! Your one stop shop for university societies in Glasgow.

We know how difficult it can be to find the right society for you, especially with their being so many social media forms. So, we have put them all in one place for you! This will allow you to look up societies not just from your own university, but from other institutes in your area.

Use the search bar to input the kind of society you are looking for and browsed the options from those in your area until you find the right society for you. Each society have their own page with up-to-date contact details and information about upcoming events.

Still unsure? Take a look at reviews left by other students to get a feel for the atmosphere before you go. Starting a new society can be nerve-wracking so Society Search is here to make the process as easy as possible for you.

Want to add your society to our ever-growing database? Sign up as a society admin and add your society today!
